## Atom sub-types (optional)

Add to an atom's `tags:` list to enable finer graph colouring within the atom type.
A `#framework` atom and a `#theorem` atom are both `type: atom` — these tags distinguish them.

- `framework` — a structured model for thinking about a domain
- `heuristic` — a practical rule of thumb derived from experience
- `cognitive-bias` — a systematic deviation from rational judgment
- `theorem` — a formally provable or empirically established result
- `school-of-thought` — a named intellectual movement or tradition
- `mechanism` — a causal process explaining how something works
- `concept` — a named idea that organises a domain without being a mechanism or theorem
- `person` — a historically significant individual (distinct from author nodes)
- `event` — a historically significant occurrence
- `tool` — a method, technique, or instrument applied to problems
